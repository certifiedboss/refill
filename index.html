ABOUT REFILL

Refill is a mobile gas refill/sales app that enables customers nationwide to order for cooking gas (LPG) and buy cylinder from their smartphones. Get the app for Android and IOS phones.

In one click a rider is there to pick up your cylinder (on custom delivery) or have a filled cylinder delivered to you (on express deliver).

HOW TO GET REFILLED

1. DOWNLOAD THE APP

2. ORDER

3. SELECT DELIVERY OPTION

4. GET REFILLED

5. PAY AND RATE

WHAT REFILL OFFERS

DON'T JUST GET REFILLED...The aim is to see that no time is lost when your cooking gas finishes. You can still get other things going while we do the running around to have your gas refilled and delivered to you. Using Refill gives you that relief effortlessly.

A RELIEF TO GETTING REFILLED

You can use Refill from any where you are and you can order for not just you but friends and family.

GUARANTEED DELIVERY

Contact details of delivery rider are displayed and you can rate them at the end of the delivery.

CARD OR CASH

Whichever way you choose to pay, just make the switch, cash or card, you are in control. Add your card to the app and worry less or pay on delivery.
